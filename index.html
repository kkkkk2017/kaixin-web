PhD Candidate (Data Science), RMIT, Melbourne

Keywords: Cognitive Bias, Information Retrieval, Wearable Sensors, User Study, Data Science

I would use 3 words to describe myself, "Hybrid, Collaborative, Independent". My research topic looks at users' cognitive bias in the interactive information seeking with physiological sensing. The physiological data include brain activity, electrodermal activity, eye, motion, etc. I enjoy doing multi-disciplinary and human-centered research. And I often collaborate with different people. Don't hesitate to connect with me.
